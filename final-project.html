Catching Legends

An ML analysis on identifying legendary Pokemon

Grace Stewart

Chaminade University

2025-05-05

Background

Pokemon is a beloved franchise featuring various creatures that players can collect and battle

  • Each Pokemon has its own unique set of typings, statistics, and classifications

  • Some are categorized as “legendary” Pokemon

    • Legendary Pokemon are considered far more rare and exclusive

Research Question

What attribute can best predict a legendary Pokemon?

  • This analysis will explore whether or not Pokemon classified as “legendary” can be predicted through various key factors and attributes.

Data

The data being used is from “The Complete Pokemon Dataset.” The data was put together by user Rounak Banik and procured from Kaggle. It includes 802 Pokemon from Generations 1-7 (also known as the first 7 mainline games).

  • https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rounakbanik/pokemon

Comparisons

Logistic Regression

  • Capture rate is strongest predictor

  • Some stats (HP, Speed, Defense, Sp. Attack) are significant

  • Strong overall fit (60% pseudo R²)

    • Potential overfitting

Decision Tree

  • Capture rate is what cleanly splits the data the most

    • Pokemon with <20 capture rate are highly likely to be legendary

  • Model strongly classifies majority as non-legendary first
